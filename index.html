FAQ

Frequently asked questions.

Is capital guaranteed?

No. All trading involves risk. ETH Aggressive v1 is designed to control risk, not eliminate it.

Does the model work in sideways markets?

Yes. When markets are choppy or low-volatility, the engine naturally reduces trading frequency and exposure.

Can I stop at any time?

Yes. You can disable trading and close positions at any time from your exchange.

What is the recommended capital?

Around 1,000 USDT or more is recommended to mirror the backtest risk profile meaningfully.
